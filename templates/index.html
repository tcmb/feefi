What is this?

This filters your Strava Feed (the activities of people you're following) by certain criteria in order to help you find a route to ride.
People your're following are often riding nice routes, but these rides are drowning in lots of commutes and other activities in your feed. This tool helps you to find the interesting rides that you might want to ride yourself.

Limitations

It only filters the most recent 200 activities in your feed. This is a hard limitation put in place by Strava. Depending on how many people you follow and how relevant their activities are to you, this might limit the number of good results you're getting.
